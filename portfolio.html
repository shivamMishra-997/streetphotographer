About the Photographer
The street is my darkroom — shadows are my chemicals, light is my developer.

Alex Raven is a documentary street photographer based between New York and Tokyo. With over a decade spent navigating urban margins, Alex captures the raw, unguarded pulse of city life — the fleeting glances, lonely corridors, and electric crowds that define our collective urban experience.

Shooting exclusively in black and white, the work strips away colour's distraction to reveal pure form, texture, and emotion. Each photograph is a negotiation between anonymity and intimacy, absence and presence.
